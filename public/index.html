Hope for Ethiopia Atlanta Inc. is Not-for-profit, Non-political, Non-governmental and charity> based organization registered under the Georgia State law as a domestic Not-for-Profit Corporation in 2009. Since then, its mission has been all about supporting fellow Ethiopians that have been affected by natural and man-made disaster and support disadvantaged Ethiopian children.

Hope for Ethiopia - Atlanta supports disadvantaged Ethiopian children by providing them with food, shelter and access to basic education. It also provides educational materials such as books, pens, pencils, school uniforms and computers to local primary and secondary schools and their libraries....
